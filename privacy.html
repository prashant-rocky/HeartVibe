Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 28, 2025

Welcome to HeartVibe! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit HeartVibe.com or interact with our content. By using our website, you agree to the terms outlined below.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information from our users:

  • Personal Information: such as your name and email address when you subscribe to our newsletter, comment on blogs, or contact us directly.
  • Non-Personal Information: such as browser type, device information, referral source, and pages visited — collected automatically through analytics tools and cookies.
  • User Interactions: comments, likes, and feedback submitted on our blogs or social media channels.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Improve the content, layout, and user experience of HeartVibe.
  • Send newsletters, updates, and relationship tips (only if you subscribe).
  • Respond to questions, feedback, or inquiries submitted through our contact form.
  • Analyze site traffic and engagement through tools like Google Analytics.

3. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

HeartVibe uses cookies and similar technologies to enhance your browsing experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us remember preferences, track site traffic, and improve content relevance.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time, but certain features of the site may not function properly if you do.

4. Email Communications

When you subscribe to our newsletter or updates, we collect your email address. We use this information only to send relationship tips, new blog updates, and relevant announcements. You can unsubscribe from any email by clicking the “unsubscribe” link at the bottom of our messages.

5. Comments and User Content

When you leave a comment on our blog, we may collect your name, email address, and IP address for spam detection and moderation. Please avoid sharing sensitive or personal information in comments, as they are publicly visible once approved.

6. Third-Party Services

HeartVibe may use third-party services such as Google Analytics, Mailchimp, or social media plugins. These services may collect information as described in their respective privacy policies. We do not control or take responsibility for the data practices of these third parties.

7. Data Protection

We take appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. While no online platform is completely secure, we strive to use industry-standard safeguards to keep your data safe.

8. Children’s Privacy

HeartVibe is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect or store personal information from individuals under the age of 18. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately for removal.

9. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request corrections or deletions of your information.
  • Withdraw consent for email communications at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the details below.

10. Changes to This Policy

HeartVibe may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last updated” date above. We encourage you to review this page regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
